How to play

Choose any two digit number, add together both digits and then subtract the total from original number.

Look at the chart and find relevant symbol for that number. Concentrate on the symbol and when you have it clearly on your mind click on the rectangle.

For example if you choose 71; 7 + 1 = 8, 71 minus 8 will give you the answer
